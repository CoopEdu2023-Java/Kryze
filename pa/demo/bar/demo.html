作品介绍

每个中国人在出生时都会被分配一组公民身份号码（CIDN），它完全代表了每个人在社会中的身份。这份报告的话题涉及身份证号码，涉及两个主要问题：截至目前中国政府发布的CIDN的最大数量，以及目前正在使用的有效CIDN的估计数量。研究深入了解了CIDN结构演变的背景信息，分析了其组成部分的变化，经由假设与计算后，解答了这两个问题。

Each Chinese is assigned with a set of fixed digits called their Citizenship Identity Number (CIDN) the moment they are born, and it represents their identity in the society. This report focuses on Chinese ID numbers and aims to answer two main questions: the maximum number of CIDNs issued by the Chinese government to date and the estimated number of valid CIDNs currently in use.
